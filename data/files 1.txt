1. Who is the father of the Indian Navy?
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj is regarded as the Father of the Indian Navy for his visionary creation of a naval fleet to protect maritime borders.

2. When is Navy Day in India?
December 4th, commemorating Operation Trident in 1971.

3. What is the Indian Navy motto?
"Sam no Varunah" (May the Lord of Water be auspicious unto us).

4. What is an Aircraft Carrier?
A warship serving as a mobile airbase with a full-length flight deck for launching and recovering aircraft.

5. Name India's active aircraft carriers.
INS Vikramaditya and the indigenous INS Vikrant.

6. What is a Stealth Frigate?
A versatile warship designed with radar-evading hull profiles for multi-mission tasks.

7. What is a Destroyer?
A fast, high-endurance warship equipped with heavy guided missile matrices for battle group defense.

8. What is the function of the Andaman and Nicobar Command?
It is India's first integrated tri-services command combining Army, Navy, and Air Force assets.

9. What is the role of MARCOS?
They are the elite special forces of the Indian Navy trained for maritime counter-terrorism and amphibious warfare.

10. What is the significance of Operation Trident?
A successful 1971 anti-ship attack on Karachi harbor using anti-ship missiles.

11. LM2500 high-pressure turbine vibration limit?
Operational limits are below 2.0 mils; anomalies start at 2.5 mils.

12. Protocol for LM2500 vibration over 2.5 mils?
Reduce shaft RPM, engage auxiliary cooling, and inspect compressor blades for damage.

13. Causes of SPY-6 radar thermal overload?
Coolant flow dropping below 45 GPM.

14. MK 41 VLS deluge activation trigger?
Module temperature sensors exceeding 70 degrees Celsius.

15. How to troubleshoot MK 41 deluge valve failure?
Test solenoid voltage, check water supply pressure, and verify manual override status.

16. Degaussing purpose?
Neutralizes the ship's magnetic signature to prevent magnetic mine detonation.

17. How to fix CPP (Controllable Pitch Propeller) loss of pressure?
Lock shaft pitch, switch to backup pump, and check cylinder seals.

18. What is cavitation?
The formation of propeller bubbles leading to structural pitting and acoustic noise.

19. How to detect cavitation?
By monitoring hull vibration and passive sonar noise signatures.

20. Bilge pump diagnostic check?
Verify suction lines and float switch operation.

21. Gas turbine fuel pressure drop cause?
Clogged filter or failing fuel lift pump.

22. Degaussing panel error fix?
Check current output for impedance mismatches.

23. Auxiliary cooling pump criteria?
Engage during high-load operations to prevent gas turbine thermal runaway.

24. Sonar dome biofouling impact?
Attenuates acoustic signals; requires physical hull inspection.

25. Reduction gearbox function?
Steps down high turbine RPM to optimal propeller rotation speeds.

26. Radar waveguide noise troubleshooting?
Inspect for loose connections or moisture ingress.

27. Integrated electric propulsion concept?

28. Saltwater in fuel impact?

33. SPY-6 coolant manifold air lock fix?
Open bleed valves at the highest point of the loop.

35. Turbine inlet temperature limit impact?
Exceeding limits causes permanent blade distortion and engine failure.

36. Fire main pressure loss diagnostic?

49. Radar transmitter arcing cause?
